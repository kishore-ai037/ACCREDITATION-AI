# 🎓 AccrediAI – AI-Powered Accreditation Management Platform

## 📖 Overview

**AccrediAI** is a comprehensive AI-powered accreditation management platform designed to simplify, automate, and modernize the accreditation process for higher education institutions. The platform centralizes institutional data, evidence collection, compliance tracking, document management, and reporting for **NAAC**, **NBA**, and **IQAC** under a single intelligent ecosystem.

Unlike traditional accreditation software that focuses only on document storage, **AccrediAI** functions as an intelligent accreditation assistant by automatically organizing evidence, suggesting compliance mappings, tracking institutional progress, and generating reports required for accreditation cycles.

# 🚀 Project Vision

AccrediAI aims to become a complete digital accreditation ecosystem where colleges can manage their entire accreditation lifecycle—from institutional setup to evidence collection, AI-assisted compliance mapping, audit management, and final report generation—through a single intelligent platform.

Instead of replacing paperwork with digital folders, **AccrediAI** transforms accreditation into an automated, data-driven, and AI-assisted workflow that reduces manual effort, improves compliance accuracy, and enables institutions to prepare confidently for accreditation assessments.
